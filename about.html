About Bagacay National High School

Learn more about who we are and what we stand for

🏫 School Overview

Bagacay National High School is a public secondary school committed to delivering quality education to the youth of Bagacay and neighboring communities. Since its establishment, the school has grown in population and performance, producing graduates who are academically prepared and socially responsible.

🎯 Mission

To provide accessible and quality education that nurtures lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible citizens through a safe and inclusive learning environment.

🌟 Vision

Bagacay National High School envisions itself as a leading educational institution that empowers learners to excel academically and contribute positively to society.

💡 Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Respect
  • Commitment
  • Collaboration
